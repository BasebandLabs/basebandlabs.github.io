Baseband Labs

We're building Baseband, next-gen sequencing infrastructure for web3.

We're a diverse team of builders excited by the potential of peer-to-peer software. Tired of the complexity web3 has become known for, we're here to build a decentralized internet for everyone, not just the techno-wizards.

Released in early 2025, our Snowpack whitepaper1 describes the first sequencing protocol capable of reaching internet-scale. Baseband2 builds upon Snowpack, providing developers a platform to break out of the walled gardens of specific blockchains without giving up on the core values of decentralization.
